Fortress Defense Setups: Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Fortress → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Fortress got a full rework in late 2025, and the site plans that hold it now are built around one thing: cameras the attack never finds. Valkyrie is essential on three of the four sites for a reason. Here's the site-by-site plan from the strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Bedroom / Commander Office (2F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Wamai to cover the projectile lanes, Alibi hunting info from the connectors, Azami anchoring site.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit secures the Bedroom wall — and put him on the Commander wall too, because the exterior breach is the usual hard-breach line.

Get the read: Valkyrie hides Black Eyes outside the site for early rotation and entry reads.

Callouts: Bedroom, Commander Office, Courtyard, Watchtower, Main Stairs, East Wing.

Dormitory / Briefing Room (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Maestro (intel). Add Aruni to cover the projectile lanes, Caveira hunting info from the connectors, Doc anchoring site.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid secures the Dormitory wall; a Mira between Dormitory and Briefing reads the central push.

Deny the plant: Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Dormitory plant spots and the main entry corridor.

Callouts: Dormitory, Briefing Room, Courtyard, Watchtower, Main Stairs, East Wing.

Kitchen / Cafeteria (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Aruni to cover the projectile lanes, Pulse hunting info from the connectors, Goyo anchoring site.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid secures the Kitchen wall against the breach.

Hold the right room: anchor Cafeteria — Kitchen has too many entry vectors; Valkyrie's hidden cams call the push before it commits.

Callouts: Kitchen, Cafeteria, Courtyard, Watchtower, Main Stairs, East Wing.

Hammam / Sitting Room (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Aruni for utility denial, Smoke for sustain and plant denial, Ela playing off-site for picks and timing pressure.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electrifies the Hammam wall against the breach.

Deny the plant: anchored 4, roaming 1 — a Maestro Evil Eye in Hammam covers both Sitting Room plant spots while Ela pressures the flanks.

Callouts: Hammam, Sitting Room, Courtyard, Watchtower, Main Stairs, East Wing.

The habit that actually holds Fortress

Post-rework Fortress is a cam war. Three of the four site plans run Valkyrie as essential because the map's long approaches — Courtyard, Watchtower, East Wing — give hidden Black Eyes enormous value: the attack has to cross open ground, and every crossing you see is a rotation you win. Place the cams outside the site, anchor the tighter room, and make the attack walk into the read.
