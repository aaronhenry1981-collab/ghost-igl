Bank Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Bank → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Bank is the oldest wall war in Siege: every site has one reinforced wall the attack wants open, and the defense that keeps it closed wins on time alone. Basement is its own game — a Mira window and Smoke on Tunnel turn it into a fortress. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

CEO Office / Executive Lounge (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Mute to cover drone holes and reinforced walls, Vigil to roam and waste attacker time, Valkyrie for cameras in the key hallways.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electroclaw the CEO wall from the floor below or behind cover; Mute jammers on the CEO wall and drone entry points back it up.

Deny the plant: Smoke canisters in the doorways stop the plant; Valkyrie cameras in elevator hall and server room call the push before it lands.

Callouts: CEO, Executive Lounge, Janitor, Skylight, Server, Spiral Stairs.

Open Area / Staff Room (1F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Maestro (intel/denial). Add Jäger for utility denial, Alibi to roam basement and lobby, Thunderbird for healing in the high-traffic rotation spots.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit tricking on the reinforced wall is critical — listen for the Hibana or Thermite and pop the battery on the sound.

Deny the plant: Maestro Evil Eyes covering the plant spots; Jäger ADS near doorways and windows eat the flashbangs; Alibi prismas in the common push paths slow the commit.

Callouts: Open Area, Staff Room, Electrical, Admin, Lobby.

Teller's Office / Archives (1F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Lesion (intel/delay). Add Jäger for utility denial, Pulse playing below for vertical intel and C4, Aruni gates on the critical doorways.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit-trick the teller wall to deny the Ace open from lobby.

Deny the plant: Lesion Gu mines at every entry point give the early warning; Jäger ADS cover windows and doors; Pulse reads below the floor for the C4 punish on the planter.

Callouts: Teller's, Archives, Lobby, Main Stairs, Admin.

Lockers / CCTV Room (B)

Anchor with: Mira (intel/anchor), Smoke (area denial). Add Mozzie to capture attacker drones for counter-intel, Ela to roam topside and waste time, Castle to delay the server stairs push.

Reinforce / trick: Mira Black Mirror on the server/lockers wall for intel on the breach; Castle barricades on the key doorways slow everything through server stairs.

Deny the plant: Smoke canisters deny the Tunnel push and the plant; Mozzie Pests in garage and tunnel entrances blind the attack before it starts.

Callouts: Lockers, CCTV, Server Stairs, Tunnel, Garage.

The habit that actually holds Bank

Bank is wall denial first, basement discipline second. Every site's round hinges on one reinforced wall — CEO, Open Area, Tellers, Server — and a Bandit or Kaid who wins that trick buys the whole defense 60 seconds of attacker confusion. Downstairs, the Mira window plus Smoke on Tunnel is the strongest default hold on the map. Keep the wall shut, keep the gas for the plant, and let the roam bleed the clock.
