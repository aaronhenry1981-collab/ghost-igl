Nighthaven Labs Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Nighthaven Labs → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Nighthaven Labs rewards the defense that sees the attack coming: hidden Valkyrie cams outside the site, Mira mirrors on the connectors, and a Caveira making every flank clear cost double. Get the read, then hold the wall. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Command / Server (2F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Wamai to catch grenades, Caveira cycling flanks to bait isolated entries, Azami anchoring Server as the trade lane.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit denies the breach on Command and tricks the Server reinforced wall against Hibana pellet timings.

Get the read: Valkyrie hides Black Eyes outside the site for early rotation and entry reads — the cam they never find is the round-winner.

Callouts: Command, Server, Reception, Loading Bay, Side Stairs, Maintenance.

Control / Storage (1F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Mira (intel). Add Jäger to protect the anchors from grenades, Caveira to roam and delay, Goyo for sustain or last-second plant denial.

Reinforce / trick: Mute electrifies the Control wall against the hard breach.

Get the read: Mira Black Mirror between Control and Storage gives sightlines on the standard push before it commits.

Callouts: Control, Storage, Reception, Loading Bay, Side Stairs, Maintenance.

Kitchen / Cafeteria (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Mira (intel). Add Aruni to protect the anchors, Caveira to roam and delay, Lesion for sustain and last-second plant denial.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electrifies the Kitchen wall; Mira Black Mirror between Kitchen and Cafeteria reads the incoming push.

Roam smart: roam toward Hangar — most attacker droning starts from there, and killing the drones kills the plan.

Callouts: Kitchen, Cafeteria, Reception, Loading Bay, Side Stairs, Maintenance.

Tank / Assembly (B)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Jäger to cover the projectile lanes, Vigil hunting info from the connectors, Lesion anchoring site.

Reinforce / trick: Mute secures the Tank wall — and reinforce the Maintenance hatches, the main vertical threat into Tank.

Play intel-first: Valkyrie's hidden Black Eyes outside the site call the rotation before the attack ever reaches the basement.

Callouts: Tank, Assembly, Reception, Loading Bay, Side Stairs, Maintenance.

The habit that actually holds Nighthaven Labs

Nighthaven defense lives on cams the attack never clears. Every site plan here starts with intel placed off-site — a hidden Black Eye, a mirror on the connector — because the site itself is holdable once you know which door the push is coming through. Place the intel first, reinforce the hatches, and let your roamer turn their slow clear into a lost clock.
