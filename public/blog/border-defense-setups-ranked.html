Border Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Border → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Border is the Armory wall war and everything that orbits it: every site here has one reinforced wall the attack has to open, and a defender committed to tricking it can flat-out steal rounds. Layer early-warning intel on top and the attack runs out of clock before it runs out of walls. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Armory Lockers / Archives (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Mute to support the wall and deny drone intel, Vigil to roam Offices and East Balcony, Maestro Evil Eyes for intel on the plant.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electroclaw the Armory wall from behind cover inside the site; Mute jammers on the wall and drone entry points back it up.

Deny the plant: Smoke canisters hold the Archives doorway against the late-round plant; Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Archives plant spots.

Callouts: Armory Lockers, Archives, East Balcony, Main Stairs, CCTV, Offices.

Workshop / Ventilation Room (1F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Jäger to protect the anchors from flashbangs and projectiles, Ela to roam Main Lobby and Central Stairs with Grzmot mines, Thunderbird for sustain near the Workshop–Ventilation rotation.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit-trick the Workshop wall to deny Hibana every round — it's the whole site.

Deny the plant: Smoke canisters hold the Supply Corridor against the late plant; Jäger ADS near the Workshop windows and doorways strip the execute utility.

Callouts: Workshop, Ventilation, Parking Lot, Main Lobby, Supply Corridor, Central Stairs.

Customs Inspection / Supply Room (1F)

Anchor with: Mira (intel/anchor), Smoke (area denial). Add Mozzie to capture attacker drones for counter-intel, Alibi to roam Main Lobby and Waiting Room with prismas, Castle to delay the Central Stairs and Break Room pushes.

Reinforce / trick: Mira Black Mirror on the Customs/Supply wall gives the read on the exterior push; Castle barricades on Central Stairs and Break Room entries slow everything else.

Deny the plant: Smoke holds the Passport Check corridor and denies the plant; Mozzie Pests in the supply corridor and Break Room blind the droning before the execute.

Callouts: Customs Inspection, Supply Room, Passport Check, Central Stairs, Main Lobby, Waiting Room.

Bathroom / Tellers (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Lesion (intel/delay). Add Jäger to protect the site from flashbangs and projectiles, Valkyrie cameras in Offices and East Balcony for early rotation intel, Aruni gates to slow the push.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electroclaw the Tellers wall from behind the counter; Aruni Surya gates on the Offices corridor and Main Stairs tax every entry.

Deny the plant: Lesion Gu mines at every entry point give early warning on the pushes; Jäger ADS cover the East Balcony windows and doorways; Valkyrie cameras call the rotation before it commits.

Callouts: Bathroom, Tellers, East Balcony, Main Stairs, Offices, CCTV, Detention.

The habit that actually holds Border

Border defense starts and ends at the reinforced wall. Armory, Workshop, and Tellers are all decided by whether your Kaid or Bandit keeps the breach shut — win the trick and the attack has to funnel through corridors your Smoke, mines, and gates already own. Put intel on the flanks (Valkyrie, Lesion, Mozzie), keep the gas in your pocket for the plant, and make the attack pay for the wall twice.
