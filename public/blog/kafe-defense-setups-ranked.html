Kafe Dostoyevsky Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Kafe Dostoyevsky → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Kafe Dostoyevsky is Siege's vertical map: three floors stacked on top of each other, and almost every attack wins by opening the ceiling above your head. The defense that reinforces overhead, holds the stairs, and keeps its wall tricks alive turns Kafe into a grind the attack can't afford. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Bar / Cocktail Lounge (3F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Jäger to protect the site from flashbangs and Capitão bolts, Alibi to roam Freezer and Cigar Shop with prismas, Aruni gates to slow the Main Entrance and White Stairs pushes.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electroclaw the Bar wall from behind cover in Cocktail Lounge; Aruni Surya gates on Main Entrance and White Stairs make every entry cost utility.

Deny the plant: Smoke denies the plant at the Main Entrance doorway and the bar counter area; Jäger ADS covering Main Entrance and the windows eat the execute utility.

Callouts: Bar, Cocktail Lounge, Main Entrance, Red Stairs, White Stairs, Freezer.

Reading Room / Fireplace Hall (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Jäger and Wamai to stack utility denial against Capitão fire and grenades, Vigil to roam White Stairs and Cigar Shop and waste attacker time.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electroclaw the Reading Room wall from behind the piano — safe from the EMP-then-breach timing.

Deny the plant: Smoke canisters on the Fireplace doorways in the last 30 seconds; Jäger ADS cover the windows and White Stairs entry; Wamai Mag-NETs near the Reading Room windows.

Callouts: Reading Room, Fireplace Hall, White Stairs, Red Stairs, Cigar Shop, Piano.

Mining Room / Fireplace Hall (2F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Maestro (intel/denial). Add Mute to deny drone intel and support the wall, Ela to roam Freezer and White Stairs with Grzmot mines, Lesion for early warning on every entry.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit-trick the Mining Room wall to deny the Hibana open from Cigar Balcony; Mute jammers support the wall denial and cover the drone holes.

Deny the plant: Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Fireplace Hall plant spots and the Pillar angle; Lesion Gu mines at the entries and stairwells call the execute before it starts.

Callouts: Mining Room, Fireplace Hall, White Stairs, Red Stairs, Freezer, Pillar.

Kitchen / Bakery (1F)

Anchor with: Smoke (area denial), Mira (intel). Add Jäger to protect the Mira window and anchor positions, Vigil to roam above site and deny the vertical setup, Thunderbird for healing near the rotation between sites.

Reinforce / trick: Mira Black Mirror on the Kitchen/Bakery wall gives the read on the push through the corridor.

Deny the plant: Smoke holds the Bakery Corridor and denies the plant; Jäger ADS covering the Mira window and the entries keep the execute honest; Thunderbird Kona station near the rotation point keeps anchors alive.

Callouts: Kitchen, Bakery, Bakery Corridor, Prep Area, Freezer, Red Stairs.

The habit that actually holds Kafe

Kafe is won and lost above your head. Every site's strat notes say the same thing: the attack wants the ceiling — Mining and Cocktail over Cooking, the roof over Fireplace, Reading Room over Kitchen — and the stairs are how they get there. Reinforce overhead where you can, put your roamers on White Stairs and Red Stairs to tax every rotation, and keep the wall trick alive so the attack has to win vertical AND horizontal to touch the bomb.
