Presidential Plane Defense Setups: Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Presidential Plane → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Presidential Plane is a corridor in the sky: two staircases, one long hallway spine, and sites that reward the defense that knows which end the push is coming from. Cams and anchors win here; over-roaming dies in the aisles. Here's the site-by-site plan from the strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Meeting Room / Office (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Echo (intel). Add Aruni to deny projectile spam, Lesion to sustain the anchors, Ela off-site for early picks that force slow play.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid on the Meeting Room wall; a Mira on the Meeting wall reads the standard front-stair push.

Play intel-first: Echo cams cover the site entries and feed the rotation calls.

Callouts: Meeting Room, Office, Tarmac, Front Stairs, Aft Stairs, Galley.

Security Room / Cargo Hold (1F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Jäger for utility denial, Thunderbird for sustain, Vigil playing off-site for picks and timing pressure.

Reinforce / trick: Mute electrifies the Security Room wall against the breach.

Hold the right room: anchor Security — Cargo has too many entry vectors to hold solo; Valkyrie's hidden Black Eyes call the push early.

Callouts: Security Room, Cargo Hold, Tarmac, Front Stairs, Aft Stairs, Galley.

Kitchen / Stateroom (1F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Wamai to protect the anchors from grenades, Alibi to roam and delay, Thunderbird for sustain or last-second plant denial.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit-trick the Kitchen exterior wall — it's the common hard-breach line.

Get the read: Valkyrie hides Black Eyes outside the site for early rotation and entry reads.

Callouts: Kitchen, Stateroom, Tarmac, Front Stairs, Aft Stairs, Galley.

Work Room / Lobby (1F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Echo (intel). Add Jäger to protect the anchors from grenades, Caveira to roam and delay, Lesion for sustain or last-second plant denial.

Reinforce / trick: Mute electrifies the Work Room wall against the hard breach.

Deny the plant: a Maestro Evil Eye in Lobby covers both plant spots cleanly; Echo cams feed the rotation calls.

Callouts: Work Room, Lobby, Tarmac, Front Stairs, Aft Stairs, Galley.

The habit that actually holds Presidential Plane

Plane gives the attack exactly two ways in — Front Stairs and Aft Stairs — and every site plan in the book exploits that: put a cam on each staircase and the push announces itself. Anchor the room with fewer doors, keep one player taxing the flank, and never trade wide in the center aisle where the attack holds the long angle. Know which staircase; win the round.
