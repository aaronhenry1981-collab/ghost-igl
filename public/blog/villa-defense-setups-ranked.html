Villa Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Villa → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Villa is one of the best-designed maps in ranked, and it rewards intel over raw aim. Long sightlines, roam-heavy layouts, and hatches everywhere mean the defender who reinforces the floor and gets a Mira read usually wins. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Aviator / Games Room (2F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Jäger, Vigil to roam Study/Main Stairs, Wamai for extra denial.

Reinforce + trick: Bandit-trick the Aviator wall to deny the hard breach.

Deny the plant: Smoke in the Games Room doorways; Wamai Mag-NETs near site to eat flashes and nades.

Callouts: Aviator, Games Room, Study, Astronomy, Main Stairs, Vault.

Trophy / Statuary (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Maestro (intel/denial). Add Mute, Ela to roam Red Stairs/Bathroom, Melusi to slow.

Reinforce: Kaid electroclaw the Trophy wall and the overhead hatch — the vertical is real here.

Deny the plant: Maestro Evil Eyes on the Statuary plant spots; Melusi Banshees on Classical hallway and Main Hall.

Callouts: Trophy, Statuary, Red Stairs, Balcony, Classical, Bathroom.

Kitchen / Dining (1F)

Anchor with: Mira (intel/anchor), Smoke (area denial). Add Thunderbird for sustain, Vigil to roam 2F/Red Stairs, Castle to delay.

Reinforce: Mira Black Mirror between Kitchen and Pantry for intel on the Mudroom push.

Deny the plant: Smoke in the Dining Room doorways.

Callouts: Kitchen, Dining, Mudroom, Pantry, Library Hallway, Red Stairs.

Living Room / Library (1F)

Anchor with: Mira (intel/anchor), Kaid (anti-breach). Add Jäger to protect the Mira window, Alibi to roam Kitchen/2F, Azami to patch.

Reinforce + trick: Kaid electroclaw the Library wall and the floor hatch; Mira Black Mirror on the Living Room wall for sightlines into Main Hall.

Seal the gaps: Azami Kiba barriers to shut unexpected breaches and rotations.

Callouts: Living Room, Library, Garden, Main Hall, Fireplace, Piano.

The habit that actually holds Villa

Villa is an intel and hatch map. The rounds you lose are the ones where you had no read on the push (no Mira, no cam) or you left a hatch/wall un-reinforced and got opened from an angle you didn't expect. Get your Mira window, reinforce the floor, and use your roamers to gather intel, not just to hunt kills — then collapse on the plant with information.
