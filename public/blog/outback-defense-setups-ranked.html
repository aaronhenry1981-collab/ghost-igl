Outback Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Outback → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Outback defense revolves around one piece of geography: Bushranger. Attacker map control starts there on almost every site, so the defense that contests it — or at least watches it — sees the push coming a full rotation early. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Laundry / Piano (2F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Maestro (intel). Add Wamai for utility denial, Mozzie for sustain and drone denial, Alibi playing off-site for picks and timing pressure.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit electrifies the Laundry wall against the breach.

Roam smart: anchored 4, roaming 1 — Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Laundry plant spots while Alibi roams Bushranger, where most attacker map control starts.

Callouts: Laundry, Piano Room, Reception, Bushranger, Convenience Store, Compressor Room.

Party / Office (2F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Maestro (intel). Add Jäger to catch grenades, Caveira cycling flanks to bait isolated entries, Doc anchoring Office as the trade lane.

Reinforce / trick: Mute denies the breach on Party Room; a Mira on the Office/Party wall reads the standard split push.

Deny the plant: Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Party Room plant spots and the main entry corridor.

Callouts: Party Room, Office, Reception, Bushranger, Convenience Store, Compressor Room.

Green Bedroom / Red Bedroom (1F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Maestro (intel). Add Jäger to deny projectile spam, Lesion to sustain the anchors, Alibi off-site for early picks.

Reinforce / trick: Mute on the Green Bedroom wall shuts the breach.

Hold the right room: anchor Red Bedroom — Green has too many soft walls to defend; Maestro Evil Eyes cover the plant spots and entry corridor.

Callouts: Green Bedroom, Red Bedroom, Reception, Bushranger, Convenience Store, Compressor Room.

Mechanic Shop / Kitchen (1F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Maestro (intel). Add Jäger to catch grenades, Ela cycling flanks to bait isolated entries, Mozzie anchoring Kitchen as the trade lane.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit-trick the Mechanic exterior wall — it's a common Hibana target; Mute denies the breach on Mechanic Shop.

Deny the plant: Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Mechanic Shop plant spots and the main entry corridor.

Callouts: Mechanic Shop, Kitchen, Reception, Bushranger, Convenience Store, Compressor Room.

The habit that actually holds Outback

Watch Bushranger. Every site plan on this map sends the roamer there because that's where the attack builds its map control — and an attack that loses its Bushranger setup arrives at your site late, loud, and missing a drone. Pair that with Maestro glass on the plant spots and the last 30 seconds belong to you.
