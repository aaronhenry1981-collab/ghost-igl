Skyscraper Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Skyscraper → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Skyscraper defense is a time-wasting game: deny the drones, tax every window entry, and make the attack spend its clock re-clearing roamers before it ever touches site. Every site here has a wall to hold and a corridor to gas. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Tea Room / Karaoke (2F)

Anchor with: Smoke (area denial), Mute (intel denial). Add Vigil to roam Geisha and Exhibition, Jäger for utility denial, Thunderbird for sustain in the high-traffic rotations.

Deny the intel: Mute jammers on the drone holes and the Karaoke connector kill the attacker's read before the push.

Deny the plant: Smoke holds the Tea Room doorways and saves canisters for the last 30 seconds; Jäger ADS on the Geisha window and balcony doors strip the execute utility.

Callouts: Tea Room, Karaoke, Geisha, Dragon, Hallway, Exhibition.

Bedroom / Bathroom (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Lesion (intel/delay). Add Jäger to protect the site from window projectiles, Ela to roam Geisha and Main Stairs, Aruni to gate the connectors.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electroclaw on the Bedroom wall denies the hard breach.

Early warning: Lesion Gu mines on the bathroom and hallway entries call the push; Aruni gates on the bathroom door and hallway connector slow it down.

Callouts: Bedroom, Closet, Bathroom, Balcony, Geisha, Main Stairs.

Kitchen / BBQ (1F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Maestro for intel and chip damage through smoke, Vigil to roam 2F and Dragon, Wamai for utility denial at the doorways.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit-trick the Kitchen wall to deny Thermite every round.

Deny the plant: Smoke holds the BBQ corridor; Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Kitchen entry and plant spots; Wamai Mag-NETs at the Kitchen doorways and windows eat the flashes.

Callouts: Kitchen, BBQ, Pantry, Dragon, Main Stairs, Ventilation.

Work Office / Exhibition (2F)

Anchor with: Mira (intel/anchor), Smoke (area denial). Add Mozzie to steal drones for counter-intel, Pulse playing below for vertical intel and C4, Castle to delay the hallway and ventilation pushes.

Reinforce / trick: Mira Black Mirror on the Exhibition/Work Office wall gives the read on the push; Castle barricades on hallway and ventilation force soft-breach utility.

Deny the plant: Smoke canisters in the Office doorways; Mozzie Pests in the hallway and Main Stairs drone paths blind the execute.

Callouts: Work Office, Exhibition, Hallway, Main Stairs, Ventilation.

The habit that actually holds Skyscraper

Skyscraper rounds are won before the push: jam the drones, mine the entries, and keep a roamer cycling Geisha and Exhibition so the attack burns clock re-clearing rooms. If the attacker reaches your site with 90 seconds and full intel, you set up wrong — deny the information first and the gunfight comes to you on your angles.
