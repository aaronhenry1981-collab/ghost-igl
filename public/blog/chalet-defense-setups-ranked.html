Chalet Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Vertical Threats, and Anchors

Rainbow Six Siege Chalet → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Chalet is a vertical map with a dangerous basement, so the defenders who lose it are usually the ones who ignored the floor above (or below) them. Four bomb sites, each with its own breach wall and its own vertical threat. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Master Bedroom / Office (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Jäger for the balcony windows, Vigil to roam, Melusi to slow.

Reinforce + trick: Kaid electroclaw the Master Bedroom wall from the bathroom side.

Deny the plant: Smoke in the hallway doorway; Melusi Banshees on Main Stairs and the balcony door.

Callouts: Master Bedroom, Office, Balcony, Main Stairs, Back Stairs, Library.

Bar / Gaming Room (1F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Maestro (intel/denial). Add Jäger, Ela to roam Trophy/Main Stairs, Thunderbird for sustain.

Reinforce + trick: Bandit-trick the Bar wall to deny Thermite.

Watch the vertical: Buck plays from Master Bedroom above — Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Gaming Room plant and the campfire window.

Callouts: Bar, Gaming Room, Campfire, Main Entrance, Trophy Room, Connector.

Kitchen / Trophy-Dining (1F)

Anchor with: Mira (intel), Smoke (area denial). Add Jäger to protect the Mira window, Vigil to roam above (deny vertical), Lesion for early warning.

Reinforce: Mira Black Mirror between Kitchen and Dining Room for intel on the campfire push.

Deny the plant: Smoke on the Connector and Kitchen doorway.

Callouts: Kitchen, Dining Room, Campfire, Back Stairs, Connector, Wine Cellar Stairs.

Wine Cellar / Snowmobile Garage (B)

The basement — and the site where vertical from Kitchen above wins rounds.

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Smoke (area denial). Add Mute for the wall + drones, Pulse on 1F, Castle to delay.

Reinforce + trick: Bandit-trick the Snowmobile Garage wall every round.

Counter the vertical: Pulse plays 1F for C4 up through the ceiling at Buck; Smoke denies the Basement Corridor plant.

Callouts: Wine Cellar, Snowmobile Garage, Wine Cellar Stairs, Back Stairs, Basement Corridor.

The habit that actually holds Chalet

Chalet punishes one thing above all: ignoring the floor. Every site here has a vertical attacker (Buck, Sledge) who opens above or below you. Reinforce the ceiling/hatch for the site you're on, trick the breach wall, and keep a Pulse or a listen on the floor that threatens you. Then deny the plant.
