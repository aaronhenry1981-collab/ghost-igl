Stadium Bravo Defense Setups: Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Stadium Bravo → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Stadium Bravo remixes Border and Coastline rooms behind glass, and the defense that wins on it treats it like its own map: Kaid on the walls, Valkyrie cams on the crossings, and one roamer making the Concourse expensive. Here's the site-by-site plan from the strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Office / Target Room (2F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Jäger to deny projectile spam, Azami to sustain the anchors, Ela off-site for early picks that force slow play.

Reinforce / trick: Mute on the Office wall; a Mira on the Office/Target wall reads the central 2F push.

Get the read: Valkyrie hides Black Eyes outside the site for early rotation and entry reads.

Callouts: Office, Target Room, Field, Locker Room, Tunnel, Main Stairs.

Kids' Dormitory / Dormitory (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Aruni to deny projectile spam, Smoke to sustain the anchors, Pulse off-site for early picks.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid on the Kids Dormitory wall shuts the hard breach.

Roam smart: roam the Concourse — pick attackers as they cross from the Tunnel side; Valkyrie cams make the crossing readable.

Callouts: Kids Dormitory, Dormitory, Field, Locker Room, Tunnel, Main Stairs.

Piano Room / Living Room (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Aruni to deny projectile spam, Doc to sustain the anchors, Caveira off-site for early picks that force slow play.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid on the Piano Room wall; put a Bandit on the Living exterior wall — it's the common hard-breach line.

Get the read: Valkyrie hides Black Eyes outside the site for early rotation and entry reads.

Callouts: Piano Room, Living Room, Field, Locker Room, Tunnel, Main Stairs.

Piano Room / Kitchen (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Mira (intel). Add Aruni for utility denial, Mozzie for sustain and drone denial, Ela playing off-site for picks and timing pressure.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electrifies the Piano Room wall; Mira Black Mirror between Piano Room and Kitchen reads the incoming push.

Deny the plant: anchored 4, roaming 1 — a Maestro Evil Eye in Piano covers both Kitchen plant spots.

Callouts: Piano Room, Kitchen, Field, Locker Room, Tunnel, Main Stairs.

The habit that actually holds Stadium Bravo

Stadium Bravo's attack routes all run through the same arteries — Tunnel, Locker Room, Main Stairs — so the defense that cams those three lanes knows the whole plan before the first barricade breaks. Kaid is essential on three of four sites here: get the electroclaw placement right, read the crossing, and rotate before the push lands instead of after.
