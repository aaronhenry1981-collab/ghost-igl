R6 Y11S2.2 Patch: The Changes That Win Ranked Rounds

Rainbow Six Siege Y11S2.1 → Y11S2.2 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Y11S2.2 changes decisions more than it changes aim. The biggest correction is Dokkaebi: the Jegeo Payload cooldown is now 14 seconds per target, not the old 7-second global timer. The numbers below come from Ubisoft's official Y11S2.2 notes. Here is what those numbers mean when the round is live.

Dokkaebi: track the defender you called

The same defender cannot be hit again for 14 seconds, but the cooldown is tracked per target. If the first payload forces a Maestro off cams, use that opening or switch pressure to another defender. Do not treat it like one team-wide timer.

On defense, call which player was targeted and use that player's recovery window to reposition. The safe call is specific: “Maestro has 14.” A generic “Dokk is on cooldown” can be wrong because another defender may still be targetable.

Wamai and Jäger now solve different problems

Wamai: Mag-NETs charge every 20 seconds, cap at 7, and activate in 0.5 seconds. He also gains a Deployable Shield and swaps impacts for C4. That rewards a reactive anchor who stays alive and keeps adding denial through the round.

Jäger: he returns to 3 Speed / 1 Health and the 416-C recoil is softer. His ADS utility is still placed early, so the mobility buff favors finishing setup and moving before attackers establish their first hold.

The smaller timers still change executes

  • Zofia: 3 concussion and 3 impact rounds. You can spend utility on the entry and still keep a charge for the execute.
  • Rauora: D.O.M. Panel shot cooldown drops to 1 second. Consecutive panels are less awkward under pressure.
  • Melusi: Banshee radius is 4.3m. Placements get slightly more forgiving.
  • Twitch: Shock Drone refill is 28 seconds. Preserve the drone long enough to earn the next shot.
  • Lesion: Gu refill is 25 seconds. Staying alive produces traps faster.

What to change tonight

Stop repeating the old 7-second Dokkaebi call. Decide between Wamai's live-round flexibility and Jäger's set-and-move speed based on the site. If you bring Twitch or Lesion, value survival because the shorter refill only matters while your utility engine is still running.
