Tower Defense Setups: Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Tower → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Tower drops the attack in from above — Helipad and Roof Access — so defense here is about controlling descent routes, not exterior walls. Kaid anchors three of the four sites, and every plan leans on a mirror or cams to read which stairwell the push takes. Here's the site-by-site plan from the strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Gift Shop / Lantern Room (2F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Mira (intel). Add Jäger to cover the projectile lanes, Alibi hunting info from the connectors, Smoke anchoring site.

Reinforce / trick: Mute on the Gift Shop wall; put a Bandit on the Gift Shop exterior wall — it's the common hard-breach target.

Get the read: Mira Black Mirror between Gift Shop and Lantern Room gives sightlines on the incoming push.

Callouts: Gift Shop, Lantern Room, Helipad, East Stairs, Lobby, Roof Access.

Tea Room / Bar (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Maestro (intel). Add Aruni to deny projectile spam, Azami to sustain the anchors, Alibi off-site for early picks that force slow play.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid on the Tea Room wall; a Mira between Tea and Bar reads the main-hall push.

Deny the plant: Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Tea Room plant spots and the main entry corridor.

Callouts: Tea Room, Bar, Helipad, East Stairs, Lobby, Roof Access.

Restaurant / Bird Room (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Mira (intel). Add Aruni to deny projectile spam, Mozzie to sustain and steal drones, Vigil off-site for early picks.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid on the Restaurant wall; Mira Black Mirror between Restaurant and Bird Room reads the push.

Deny the plant: a Maestro Evil Eye in Bird covers both Restaurant plant spots.

Callouts: Restaurant, Bird Room, Helipad, East Stairs, Lobby, Roof Access.

Exhibit Room / Media Center (2F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Echo (intel). Add Aruni for utility denial, Azami for sustain, Vigil playing off-site for picks and timing pressure.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electrifies the Exhibit Room wall against the breach.

Hold the right room: anchor Media Center — Exhibit has too many soft walls to defend; Echo cams cover the entries and feed the calls.

Callouts: Exhibit Room, Media Center, Helipad, East Stairs, Lobby, Roof Access.

The habit that actually holds Tower

Tower's attack comes down, not in — Helipad and Roof Access feed East Stairs and the Lobby, and those two descent routes are the whole story. Put your reads there (mirror, Evil Eye, Echo drone) and anchor the room with the fewer soft walls. Chasing kills up the stairwell is how defenders die on this map; let the attack come down into your crossfire instead.
