Kanal Defense Setups (Ranked): Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege Kanal → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

Kanal is two buildings and a bridge, and the defense that controls the bridge crossings controls the round. Every site plan in the book puts glass or a cam on the Bridge entry because that's the lane the attack has to clear. Here's the site-by-site plan from the ranked strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Server Room / Radar Room (2F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Maestro (intel). Add Jäger to deny projectile spam, Thunderbird to sustain the anchors, Alibi off-site for early picks that force slow play.

Reinforce / trick: Mute on the Server Room wall shuts the breach.

Deny the plant: Maestro Evil Eyes cover the Server Room plant spots — and an Evil Eye in Server watches the Bridge entry attackers must clear.

Callouts: Server Room, Radar Room, Lighthouse, Quay, Bridge, Boat Dock.

Security Room / Map Room (1F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Echo (intel). Add Jäger for utility denial, Doc for sustain, Caveira playing off-site for picks and timing pressure.

Reinforce / trick: Mute electrifies the Security Room wall; a Mira between Security and Map Room reads the main-stair push.

Play intel-first: anchored 4, roaming 1 — Echo cams cover the entries and feed rotation calls while Caveira taxes the flank.

Callouts: Security Room, Map Room, Lighthouse, Quay, Bridge, Boat Dock.

Coast Guard Meeting / Lounge (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Maestro (intel). Add Aruni for utility denial, Lesion for early warning and sustain, Pulse playing off-site for picks.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid electrifies the Coast Guard Meeting wall against the breach.

Roam smart: Maestro Evil Eyes cover the plant spots and entry corridor; roam East Walkway for early picks on attacker spawn-peek setups.

Callouts: Coast Guard Meeting, Lounge, Lighthouse, Quay, Bridge, Boat Dock.

Kayaks / Supply Room (B)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Jäger to cover the projectile lanes, Goyo for sustain and plant denial, Pulse playing off-site for timing pressure.

Reinforce / trick: Mute secures the Kayaks wall — and reinforce the Kayaks ceiling, the primary vertical threat into the basement.

Get the read: Valkyrie hides Black Eyes outside the site for early rotation and entry reads before the attack commits downstairs.

Callouts: Kayaks, Supply Room, Lighthouse, Quay, Bridge, Boat Dock.

The habit that actually holds Kanal

Kanal splits the attack in half: whichever building your site is in, the reinforcements have to cross the bridge or go around — and both routes are watchable. Put an Evil Eye or a cam on the Bridge, reinforce the ceiling over the basement sites, and let the geography do the work. An attack that can't cross clean can't execute on time.
