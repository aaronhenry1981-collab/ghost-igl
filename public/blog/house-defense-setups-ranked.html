House Defense Setups: Every Bomb Site, Anchors, and Reinforcements

Rainbow Six Siege House → Defense Setups 6 min read Last updated: 2026-05

House is small enough that every attacker entry is loud and every defender rotation is fast — which means intel wins it outright. Cams outside the building, mirrors on the site walls, and hatches sealed before the action phase. Here's the site-by-site plan from the strat book — the short version of what RECON6 calls live.

Master Bedroom / Closet (2F)

Anchor with: Bandit (anti-breach), Valkyrie (intel). Add Wamai to cover the projectile lanes, Vigil hunting info from the connectors, Thunderbird anchoring site.

Reinforce / trick: Bandit secures the Master Bedroom wall; a Mira on the Master/Trampoline wall reads the rooftop attack.

Get the read: Valkyrie hides Black Eyes outside the site for early rotation and entry reads.

Callouts: Master Bedroom, Closet, Front Yard, Driveway, Foyer, Backyard.

Kitchen / Living Room (1F)

Anchor with: Kaid (anti-breach), Echo (intel). Add Aruni to deny projectile spam, Lesion to sustain the anchors, Ela off-site for early picks that force slow play.

Reinforce / trick: Kaid on the Kitchen wall; put a Bandit on the Living wall — it's the common breach target for the Kitchen execute.

Play intel-first: Echo cams cover the site entries and feed the rotation calls.

Callouts: Kitchen, Living Room, Front Yard, Driveway, Foyer, Backyard.

Garage / Workshop (1F)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Mira (intel). Add Jäger to deny projectile spam, Goyo to sustain the hold, Ela off-site for early picks.

Reinforce / trick: Mute on the Garage wall; Mira Black Mirror between Garage and Workshop reads the incoming push.

Deny the plant: a Maestro Evil Eye covers both the Workshop and Garage plant spots.

Callouts: Garage, Workshop, Front Yard, Driveway, Foyer, Backyard.

Boiler / Laundry (B)

Anchor with: Mute (anti-breach), Mira (intel). Add Jäger to deny projectile spam, Goyo to sustain the anchors, Pulse off-site for early picks.

Reinforce / trick: Mute on the Boiler wall — and reinforce the Living and Kitchen hatches early, because both threaten the basement vertically.

Get the read: Mira Black Mirror between Boiler and Laundry gives sightlines on the push downstairs.

Callouts: Boiler, Laundry, Front Yard, Driveway, Foyer, Backyard.

The habit that actually holds House

House rounds are decided in the first 30 seconds of prep: seal the hatches, place the cams, set the mirror. The map is too small for slow adjustments — once the attack commits, it's on your site in seconds, so all your reads have to be pre-placed. Do the boring setup completely and the round plays itself; skip one hatch and that's the one they drop through.
