Map-wide ban recommendations (Kanal)

  • Thatcher (attack ban) — Kanal's reinforced walls on Server Room and Map Room depend on Bandit/Kaid tricking. Without Thatcher, hard breach becomes a gamble.
  • Maverick (attack ban) — Silent wall holes on key Kanal sightlines (Bridge, Quay) bypass standard defender setups. Removing him forces predictable breach lines.
  • Mira (defense ban) — Black Mirrors on Server/Bridge and Coast Meeting walls give defenders central-map intel that bypasses standard droning.
  • Maestro (defense ban) — Evil Eyes covering Lounge and Supply plant spots make late-round plants risky and stall execute timing.
