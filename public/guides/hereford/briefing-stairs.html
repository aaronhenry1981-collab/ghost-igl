Map-wide ban recommendations (Hereford Base)

  • Sledge (attack ban) — Hereford's soft floors between Master/Catwalk and Ammo/Tractor enable vertical play. Sledge is the most reliable opener.
  • Thatcher (attack ban) — Master and Kitchen reinforced walls are heavily electrified — Thatcher's EMPs are mandatory for hard breach.
  • Mira (defense ban) — Black Mirrors in Master Bedroom and Brewery cover the standard attacker push lanes. Extremely hard to clear without Twitch.
  • Lesion (defense ban) — Gu mines on Hereford's narrow connectors and stairwells provide constant intel and chip damage on every push.
