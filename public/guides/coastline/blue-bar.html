Map-wide ban recommendations (Coastline)

  • Iana (attack ban) — Her hologram safely gathers intel through Coastline's many windows and doors with zero risk. Top fragging potential.
  • Ash (attack ban) — Fast entry through Coastline's many soft walls and windows. Her speed and utility clear make her dominant on this map.
  • Maestro (defense ban) — Evil Eyes lock down Hookah, Kitchen, and Blue Bar with bulletproof intel and damage. Forces attackers to waste utility.
  • Jager (defense ban) — ADS protect every window and door on Coastline. Without them, defenders get overwhelmed by grenades and flashes.
