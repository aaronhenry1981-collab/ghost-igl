Map-wide ban recommendations (Bank)

  • Thatcher (attack ban) — Removes all electronic denial on reinforced walls — without him, hard breachers struggle to open CEO and basement walls.
  • Maverick (attack ban) — Can silently open reinforced walls without any counter. Eliminates Bandit/Kaid tricking entirely.
  • Mira (defense ban) — Black Mirrors on key walls give defenders massive intel advantage and deny safe pushes on multiple sites.
  • Valkyrie (defense ban) — Hidden cameras throughout Bank's tight hallways provide too much roamer intel and C4 kills.
