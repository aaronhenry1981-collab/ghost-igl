Map-wide ban recommendations (Fortress)

  • Thatcher (attack ban) — Fortress's reinforced walls on Commander Office and Kitchen depend on electronics. Banning Thatcher forces hard-breach gambles.
  • Buck (attack ban) — Vertical from above Hammam and Briefing is the cleanest 2F→1F pressure. Buck is the most reliable open-floor option.
  • Maestro (defense ban) — Evil Eyes covering Cafeteria and Sitting Room plant spots are extremely hard to clear. Forces attackers to spend utility before plant.
  • Goyo (defense ban) — Volcan shields in Fortress's narrow corridors deny rotations and create plant-denial fire walls. Hard counter to standard executes.
