Map-wide ban recommendations (Tower)

  • Thatcher (attack ban) — Tower's reinforced walls on Gift Shop and Restaurant depend on electronic denial. Thatcher EMP is mandatory for reliable hard breach.
  • Capitao (attack ban) — Fire bolts deny anchor positions in Tower's open exhibition rooms. Removes a key area-denial tool from execute reads.
  • Mira (defense ban) — Black Mirrors in Gift Shop and Tea Room give defenders impossible-to-counter sightlines on the standard Lobby/East push.
  • Echo (defense ban) — Yokai drones on Tower's tight site ceilings deny plants and provide constant intel during exec.
