Map-wide ban recommendations (Yacht)

  • Sledge (attack ban) — Yacht's vertical layout (Cockpit/Maps above Engine sites) favors Sledge floor-drop play. Banning him kills vertical pressure.
  • Thatcher (attack ban) — Engine Control and Cockpit reinforced walls are heavily electrified. Thatcher is mandatory for reliable hard breach.
  • Mira (defense ban) — Black Mirrors in Cafeteria and Engine Control cover Yacht's central rotation paths — extremely hard to clear with limited utility.
  • Lesion (defense ban) — Gu mines on Yacht's narrow stairwells and corridors waste attacker time and provide constant intel on rotations.
