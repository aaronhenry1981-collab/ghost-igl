Stadium Bravo — Ban Order

Optimal ban targets for Stadium Bravo ranked play. Attack bans neutralize the most dangerous attacker operators against this map; defense bans neutralize the most oppressive defender setups.

Attack Bans (ban these attackers)

Sledge — ATTACK BAN

Stadium Bravo's 2F sites have soft floors above. Sledge from Press Box dominates Office and Dorm verticals.

Thatcher — ATTACK BAN

Heavy reinforcement on Office and Living walls — without Thatcher, hard breach attempts get tricked consistently.

Defense Bans (ban these defenders)

Mira — DEFENSE BAN

Black Mirrors on Office/Target and Living/Piano walls give defenders sightlines on Tunnel/Concourse approaches.

Maestro — DEFENSE BAN

Evil Eyes covering Kitchen and Living plant spots stall executes. Forces attacker utility commitment before plant.
