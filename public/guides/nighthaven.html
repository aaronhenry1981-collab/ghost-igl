Ban recommendations

  • Thatcher (attack) — Nighthaven Labs' basement Server Room walls are heavily electrified. Without Thatcher, attackers must rely on risky Kali or Twitch plays to open critical reinforcements.
  • Flores (attack) — Flores' exploding drones clear bulletproof utility through Nighthaven's long corridors with zero risk. Banning him forces attackers to push into defender gadgets head-on.
  • Kaid (defense) — Electroclaw range lets Kaid deny hatches and walls on Server Room and Assembly simultaneously. His flexibility is critical for Nighthaven's vertical-heavy defense.
  • Smoke (defense) — Nighthaven Labs' tight chokepoints and narrow corridors make Smoke's canisters devastating for denying plant attempts on every site.
