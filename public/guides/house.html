Ban recommendations

  • Sledge (attack) — House is a vertical map — Sledge from 2F above Garage and Living dominates the standard exec.
  • Ash (attack) — House's soft walls and tight rooms favor Ash's breaching round entry pressure. Banning her slows attacker tempo.
  • Mira (defense) — Black Mirrors on Master/Trampoline and Living/Foyer walls give defenders impossible-to-counter sightlines.
  • Echo (defense) — Yokai drones on House's low ceilings deny plant attempts in Garage and basement sites.
