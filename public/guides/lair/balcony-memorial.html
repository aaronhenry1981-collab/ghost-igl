Map-wide ban recommendations (Lair)

  • Thatcher (attack ban) — Lair's basement Vault walls and 1F Workshop reinforcements rely on electronic denial. Without Thatcher, attackers must use slow Twitch or Kali plays to clear Kaid and Bandit setups.
  • Maverick (attack ban) — Maverick silently opens reinforced walls in Vault and Workshop with zero counter-play. Banning him removes the only uncounterable hard breach option on Lair's tightest sites.
  • Mira (defense ban) — Black Mirrors on Workshop connector and Surveillance hallway walls give defenders dominant intel and deny safe attacker pushes on Lair's strongest sites.
  • Smoke (defense ban) — Lair's narrow corridors and chokepoints leading into every site make Smoke's canisters devastating for denying late-round plant attempts.
