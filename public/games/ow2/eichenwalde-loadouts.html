Point A (Townsquare) —

Attack: Poke setup on Point A: Cassidy on high ground, Reinhardt bunkers behind cover, Ana amps damage. Ashe flanks Point on ult charge. Push when both supports have ult. Tavern high ground take + Bridge dive splits the defender stack.

Defense: Aggressive forward hold on Point A: Reinhardt pre-positions at Point, Cassidy on the high ground for the round-opener pick. Ana pockets the tank, Brigitte watches flank. Big Window anchor with Bridge cover denies the standard tavern push.

Payload — Castle Gate —

Attack: Layered ult chain on Payload — Castle Gate: Reinhardt ult on engage, Ana amps with Nano/Mercy boost, Cassidy ults under cover, Ashe cleans up. Brigitte preps Trans/Sound Barrier for the trade. Courtyard high ground commit with Pharah / Echo first push.

Defense: Stack Payload — Castle Gate: 5-on-objective with Reinhardt body-blocking the cap. Cassidy + Ashe cover both flanks, Ana + Brigitte chain heals. Read the pace from ult usage — full attacker ults means full commit, save your trade ults. Side Path flank watch is mandatory; rotate from Castle, not main.

Payload — Throne Room —

Attack: Standard Payload — Throne Room push: Reinhardt engages on ult cycle, Cassidy pre-positions on high ground, Ana pockets the dive. Ashe flanks from Payload; Brigitte stays behind for retreat heals. Hold the engage until ult diff is favorable. Throne Room funnel demands ult chain — commit Nano + Dragonblade or Bob.
