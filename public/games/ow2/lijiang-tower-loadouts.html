Site-By-Site Notes

Night Market —

Attack: Night Market is dive heaven — tight corridors and stall-cover everywhere. Winston bubbles a Lúcio-amped engage through the stalls onto Point. Tracer recalls off the back-line, Sojourn slides on Disruptor for the railgun pick. Stagger your dive, don't 5-man one corridor.

Defense: Anchor Night Market with 5-on-Point. Winston body-blocks the cap line. Sojourn holds the Bridge sightline. Tracer roams Side Room for the harass pick. Use Kitsune to walk the cap back if it tips past 99%.

Garden —

Attack: Garden's open layout favors poke + brawl over pure dive. Ramattra pokes from Bridge with Void Barrier, Hanzo Sonic-arrows the cap, Reaper Wraiths through Side for the support kill. Nano-Annihilate the cap on Sound Barrier trade.

Defense: Garden defense is the textbook bunker. Ramattra Void Barrier on Point, Hanzo from Bridge for the long shot, Brigitte armor-aura on the choke, Ana Sleep + Nano combo for the retake. Punish attacker tempo with Sleep-Anti on the engage.

Control Center —

Attack: Control Center is 70% dive, 30% high ground duel. Winston jumps the supports, Genji Dragonblade-pockets with Mercy boost, Soldier holds the rooftop for cleanup. Wells on the edges are kill zones — Lúcio boops on the cap pinch.

Defense: Hold Point center with Lúcio aura, Soldier on Bridge for the long sightline, Genji counter-dives the attacker support flank. Sound Barrier the cap when ult diff hits. Don't body-block the well edge — Lúcio boop = instant loss.
