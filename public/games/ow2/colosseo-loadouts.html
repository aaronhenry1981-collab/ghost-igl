Mid (Arch) —

Attack: Brawl comp on Mid: stack the choke with Orisa + Lúcio, Cassidy + Soldier: 76 hold Robot Spawn angles, Baptiste pre-heals the front-line. Walk the team forward on Lúcio speed boost. Arch high ground takeover with Pharah / Echo first commit.

Defense: Default hold on Mid: Orisa anchors choke, Cassidy + Soldier: 76 cover high ground, Baptiste + Lúcio bunker behind cover. Save ults for the attacker push commit — do not blow on a poke. Side Door flank watch — Tracer punishes high-ground stack.
