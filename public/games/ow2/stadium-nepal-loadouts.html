Site-By-Site Notes

Sanctum Point —

Attack: Sanctum is open dive paradise — Winston jump in from Catwalk, Tracer + Genji clean the back line. Side Rooms route is the secondary commit. Round 1 invest in dive Powers (Jump Pack cooldown, Pulse charge); skip Epic items until round 4.

Defense: Stack the Point cap, force dive to commit through Mei wall + Zarya bubble. Anti-dive Item priority: cooldown reduction for bubble charge, Ana scoped damage round 3. Bait Pulse with bubbled tank, then Anti the Genji blade.

Side rooms —

Attack: Brawl comp through Side Rooms — tight geometry favors shotguns. Reaper wraith into the back of the room, Rein shatter the stack, Mei wall the Point retreat. This is the round-2 round-3 anti-dive swing.

Defense: Hold Side Rooms with sight-line denial. Sigma rock the Rein entry, Ashe scope-stack the choke, Soldier suppress. If they shatter, Lúcio Sound Barrier the trade. Don't get caught in the room — it's a kill funnel for both sides.

Catwalk high ground —

Attack: Catwalk HG controls the entire Sanctum sightline. Take it first round, all rounds. Winston bubble on the climb, Genji shuriken-clear, Mercy pocket the HG damage. Defender response is Hitscan — they'll buy a Power to evict, so re-take if pushed off.

Defense: Don't let them have Catwalk free. Pre-set Orisa Javelin to evict the Winston, Ashe sniped the Mercy. If you lose HG, drop to Point — never trade up at 4HP.
