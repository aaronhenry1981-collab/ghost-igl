PUBG: Battlegrounds Squad Roles

IGL / Caller Squad shotcaller — drop, rotation, engagement calls. Reads circle + 3rd-party timing
Fragger Entry duels, top-K player. AR + close-range; gets first kills in fight
Sniper Long-range pick-off. Kar98 / SLR / Mk14; covers squad from elevated angle
Support Heals, smokes, revives. Drops boost items for fragger; mid-range backup
Scout Recon + grenade lineups. Spots enemies, flashes corners, identifies 3rd parties
Anchor Holds the compound. Defensive position; covers exit routes
