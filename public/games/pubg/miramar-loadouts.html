Site-By-Site Notes

Pecado (Center Hot Drop) —

Pushing: Pecado is Miramar's iconic hot drop. Casino + Boxing Ring buildings dominate the POI. Drop priority: Casino roof for tier-2 loot. Fragger drops first with AR; Scout drops on Boxing Ring for grenade lineups. Loot priority: AR + Sniper + level 2 helmet + 2 frags + smokes. Pecado is 75% chance of squad wipe; loot pile reward is high. Rotate via vehicle to next circle; never on foot.

Holding: Defending Pecado: 1-2 squads remain. Casino roof for vertical advantage. Sniper covers approach from far buildings. Anchor holds ground floor with shotgun. Vehicle rotation route prepared. 3rd-party timing: Pecado is mid-map; pre-position for circle close.

Pushing: Hacienda is Miramar's tier-3 loot castle. 3-5 squads land here. Drop priority: main mansion roof for tier-3 loot. Fragger drops first with AR; Sniper drops on roof for cover. Loot priority: AR + Sniper + level 3 helmet + 2 frags + smokes. Hacienda is 70% squad wipe; tier-3 loot is worth it for end-game prep.

Holding: Defending Hacienda: 1-2 squads remain. Main mansion + tower for vertical advantage. Sniper covers entrance from tower. Anchor holds main mansion. Vehicle rotation prepared. 3rd-party timing: Hacienda is iconic; mid-game hot spot.
