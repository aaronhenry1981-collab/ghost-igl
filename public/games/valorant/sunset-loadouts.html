Site-By-Site Notes

A Site (Default / Elbow) —

Attack: Standard A Site execute: Omen smokes the CT cross + Heaven, Fade flashes/recons over the choke, Phoenix entries first contact while Cypher watches the off-site flank. Plant default for the post-plant util cross. A Main take with Alley control + Elbow flash isolates Default anchor.

Defense: 1-2-2 default: solo Mid for info, two on A Site (anchor + trade), two on A Default. Cypher sets traps cross-site. Omen flexes. Listen for the Mid take — that's the rotate trigger. A Elbow anchor with Alley molly is the standard A hold; rotate from Mid Top.

B Site (Mall / Market) —

Attack: Fast hit: Omen smokes B Site entry on the buy timer, Fade flashes over, Phoenix dives first contact. Cypher trails the trade. Goal is to land before defenders rotate from off-site stack. B Mall + Market double-smoke isolates the anchor; Hookah pinch from A side.

Defense: Default 2-1-2: Cypher sets traps and watches B Site, Omen holds Mid for the early read, Phoenix plays off-site for picks. Fade recons on contact. Save ults for the retake — do not blow on a fake. B Hookah anchor with Market molly is the standard B hold; trade from Mall.
