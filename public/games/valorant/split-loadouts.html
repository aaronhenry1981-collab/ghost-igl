Site-By-Site Notes

A Site (Heaven / Tower) —

Attack: Split execute: Raze + Breach hit A Site, Omen + Killjoy take A Heaven. Both sides flash on the same count. Plant for the cross-site post-plant util cycle. A Ramps takeover with Raze nades clears Heaven anchor; Phoenix wall denies trade.

Defense: Stack A Site: 3-on-site, 2 watching Mid + A Heaven flank. Killjoy traps the choke, Omen preps retake util, Raze trades. Read pace from utility usage — full smoke commit means full execute. A Heaven AWP + Screens trade is the standard hold; Ramps molly delays the take.

B Site (Tower / Back) —

Attack: Standard B Site execute: Omen smokes the CT cross + Heaven, Breach flashes/recons over the choke, Raze entries first contact while Killjoy watches the off-site flank. Plant default for the post-plant util cross. B Tower control is mandatory — Tower flash + Mail molly forces the rotate.

Defense: 1-2-2 default: solo Mid for info, two on B Site (anchor + trade), two on B Back. Killjoy sets traps cross-site. Omen flexes. Listen for the Mid take — that's the rotate trigger. B Heaven + Mail anchor with Tower trade is the standard 2-on-B hold.
