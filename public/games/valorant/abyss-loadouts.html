Site-By-Site Notes

A Site (Heaven / Default) —

Attack: Standard A Site execute: Clove smokes the CT cross + Heaven, Tejo flashes/recons over the choke, Iso entries first contact while Killjoy watches the off-site flank. Plant default for the post-plant util cross. A Heaven control with Lobby pinch is the standard A exec; Stairs flash isolates default.

Defense: 1-2-2 default: solo Mid for info, two on A Site (anchor + trade), two on A Default. Killjoy sets traps cross-site. Clove flexes. Listen for the Mid take — that's the rotate trigger. A Heaven anchor with Stairs molly is the standard hold; gravity-fall to lower for retake.

B Site (Plat / Default) —

Attack: Mid takeover: Tejo recons Mid on round-start, Iso dashes the pick attempt, Clove smokes the rotate. Once mid is locked, split-execute B Site with Killjoy pinching from off-site. B Plat take with Stairs flash + Main pinch is the textbook B exec.

Defense: Eco / save round: stack B Site with all 5, save ults. Killjoy traps the entry choke, Iso trades. Goal is a round win or clean save — do not chase frags into the rotate. B Plat anchor with Main molly is the standard B hold; do not over-extend Lobby.
