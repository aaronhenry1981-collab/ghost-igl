Site-By-Site Notes

Opening the floor Tile damage

Offense: Dropshot opens the floor by damaging tiles with each ball hit. Hit the ball hard to damage tiles, hit harder (powered up) to break them. Aerial shots from height = max damage.

Defense: Don't let the ball hit your tiles. Clear it back to their side or take it up into the air to neutralize damage.

Breaking tiles Power shot

Offense: Once tiles are open, drop the ball through. Aerial shot straight down through the broken area = goal.

Defense: Cover the broken area. Force opponent to power up other tiles first.

Defense Floor protect

Offense: After saving, push the ball back to their side with damage. Reverse the tile damage as your counter.

Defense: Defensive recovery in Dropshot is about keeping your floor whole. Clear high, clear far.
