Site-By-Site Notes

A Site (Z / Halls) —

T: Split execute: two players hit from Z, three (Entry, AWPer, Support) take Default. Pop Dog + Z + Halls is smoked to deny the trade-cross. Both sides flash on the same count. Lurker plays Ivy + B Halls for the rotate. Pop Dog smoke + Z connector flash isolates Halls anchor; default plant for Ivy post-plant lineup.

CT: Stack A Site: 3-on-site, 2 watching mid + Z flank. AWP holds Z + Pop Dog from Heaven for the early read. Halls anchor with Z trade; Pop Dog molly delays the take. Read the pace from utility usage — full smoke commit means full execute, walk it down with HEs and flashes. Halls AWP with Z Connector trade is mandatory; do not over-extend to Pop Dog without info.

B Site (Upper / Lower) —

T: Mid-control opener: AWPer takes Mid pick attempts while Support smokes Headshot Box + Ivy + Upper. Once mid is locked, the team splits — Entry hits Site on the flash, Support and IGL trail. Lurker holds Sandwich for the A rotator pinch. Lower take splits the anchor between Site and Headshot Box; Upper Lurk pinches the retake. Plant goes down for the AWPer's post-plant cross-map angle.

CT: Eco / force-buy hold: stack B Site with all 5, save the AWP for the retake. Upper anchor with Ivy trade; Lower molly delays the rush. Trade from Site on the entry. Goal is a round win or a clean save — do not chase frags into the rotate. Headshot Box AWP + Ivy molly is the standard hold; Upper rotation through Sandwich, not Olof.
