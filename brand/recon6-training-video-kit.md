# RECON6 — Training Video Kit
### Free tips that sell the coaching. Give value, prove expertise, then point to r6coaching.com.

Training content is your best sales tool: a viewer who learns from your free tip trusts you enough to pay. Every video = one idea, 20–45s, ends with a soft CTA.

## How to shoot these cheaply (pick one, no fancy gear)
- **Voiceover over gameplay** (easiest): talk over a clip that shows the thing. Your existing footage already has the moments.
- **Text-on-gameplay**: no talking — big on-screen text making the point over a relevant clip + a trending sound.
- **Talking head**: you on cam for 15s, then cut to gameplay proving it. Highest trust, most work.
Start with voiceover-over-gameplay. It's fast and the coach's read is already the script half the time.

## The CTA (say or caption it, every time)
"That's the kind of thing my AI coach catches live while you play. First session's $20 → r6coaching.com."

## 10 training scripts (hook → teach → CTA)

**1. The 3 deaths keeping you in Silver**
Hook: "If you're hardstuck Silver, it's these 3 deaths — not your aim."
Teach: Dry-peeking with no info. Re-peeking the same angle. Dying for a kill with no trade. Show a clip of each.
CTA: standard.

**2. The sensitivity change that fixed my aim** *(your real story — authentic, high-share)*
Hook: "I didn't get better aim. I changed one setting."
Teach: "I sped up my ADS — 1x and 2.5x especially. Faster ADS turn = faster crosshair correction in the duel. Headshot rate jumped to 53%." Show before/after gunfights.
CTA: "Your aim's probably fine too. The fix is usually the read, not the flick. r6coaching.com."

**3. Drone before you push. Every time.**
Hook: "The habit that ranks you up isn't aim. It's this."
Teach: Drone every push, even a quick one. You trade 5 seconds for knowing exactly where they are. Show a push blind (death) vs a push with info (clean).
CTA: standard.

**4. Objective deaths are fine. Peek deaths are free RP.**
Hook: "There are two kinds of deaths. One's okay. One's throwing."
Teach: Dying planting/defusing/holding the objective = fine, it bought something. Dying for a greedy peek that bought nothing = free RP for them. Show one of each.
CTA: standard.

**5. Stop dry-pushing. The intel-first rule.**
Hook: "You're not losing gunfights. You're walking into them blind."
Teach: Never enter a room you have zero info on. Drone, sound, teammate cam — get one piece of intel before you commit. Show a dry push punished.
CTA: standard.

**6. You're hoarding your utility. Stop.**
Hook: "Dying with a full gadget is a loss you don't feel."
Teach: Utility unused is utility wasted. Use the flash, the frag, the breach *for the play* — not "saving it." Show a saved-then-died clip vs a used-it-won clip.
CTA: standard.

**7. The reload that gets you killed**
Hook: "This reload habit is why you die in 2v1s."
Teach: Don't reload in the open after a kill. Reload behind cover, or you're a free trade. Show it going wrong.
CTA: standard.

**8. Roaming without throwing the round**
Hook: "Roaming isn't running around dying. It's this."
Teach: A good roam costs them time and men, then rotates back before the site's a 5v3. Bad roam = you die alone off-site. Show the difference.
CTA: standard.

**9. Post-plant: where you actually stand**
Hook: "You planted. Now you're standing in the wrong spot."
Teach: Post-plant, deny the defuser — hold an angle on the plant with cover, not a hero peek. Show a good post-plant hold.
CTA: standard.

**10. "Why am I hardstuck?" — the honest answer**
Hook: "Nobody tells you WHY you're stuck. So here it is."
Teach: It's almost never aim. It's information — every drone, cam, and sound cue is a resource, and you're wasting them. The players who climb waste less info, not aim better.
CTA: "That's literally what the AI watches for. r6coaching.com, first session $20."
