### The highest-intent content you can make. People search these exact terms.

Your strat library = a video for every site, already written. Each site+side is one 30–45s video. These pull *searchers* — someone looking up "bank basement defense" wants to learn a site, which is one step from wanting coaching.

## THE FORMAT — "Strat Card"
1. **Title = the search term** (this is TikTok SEO — say it AND put it on screen): "How to hold Bank Basement (defense setup)."
2. **Hook (0–2s):** "Bank Basement on defense — here's the setup that holds it."
3. **On-screen cards**, chunked so each reads in 2–3s: Operators → Key utility → Callouts. (Text over footage of that site.)
4. **Voiceover** (you, or AI): read the setup plainly. The strat text IS your script.
5. **CTA:** "Full setups for every site, free → r6coaching.com."

## HOW TO SHOOT (cheap → premium)
- **Scalable (no new footage):** strat text as animated cards over a branded background or the site's map image + a trending sound + AI voiceover. Weaker visually, but you can make one for *every* site fast.
- **Better:** strat cards over a clip of that site from your existing footage.
- **Premium:** you play/walk the site (drone-spawn view or a round) and voiceover the setup live. Best quality, slowest.
Do premium for the top-searched sites, scalable for the long tail.

## SEO titling (do this every time)
Title, on-screen text, and caption all include the exact phrase people type: "bank ceo defense," "clubhouse church setup," "border bathroom attack." Matches TikTok search AND your now-crawlable site pages — the two reinforce each other.
