## Approach strategy

- **For each game, pitch the tool with the game-specific framing:**
  - R6/CS2/Val: "Per-round positioning + utility timing analysis."
  - OW2/MVR: "Hero swap + ult timing patterns."
  - Apex/Fortnite: "Drop spot + rotation reads."
  - Halo: "Power weapon + spawn-flip analysis."
  - The Finals: "Vault carry + Cashout contest reads."
  - CoD: "Loadout + recoil + ring rotation analysis."

- **Subject line per game:** "[Channel name] — built an [game]-specific AI VOD review, would love your honest take."

- **Don't pitch all 10 games to a single-game creator.** They'll glaze.
