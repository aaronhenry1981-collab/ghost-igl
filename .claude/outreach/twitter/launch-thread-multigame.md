# Twitter/X — Multi-Game Launch Thread (6 tweets)

## Goal

Position Recon+ as the multi-game FPS coaching platform. Audience: FPS players who've tried game-specific coaching tools and want one platform across the games they play.

## Posting strategy

- Post 1-2 weeks AFTER the R6 thread to avoid stepping on it.
- Post Wednesday-Friday morning EST.
- Use multi-game tags: #FPSCoaching, #VODReview.
- Don't tag esports orgs — they ignore.

## Engagement tactics

- Reply to every comment within 4 hours.
- Quote-RT the thread from your blog Twitter or personal if you have it.
- Don't run paid ads initially. Organic discovery only.

## Risk flags

- If users say "sounds too broad — specialization is better", reply: "Fair point. The trade-off is real. The platform is for players who actively play multiple games. Single-game players might prefer specialized tools."
- Don't dismiss critics. Engage genuinely.

## Don't

- Don't beg for retweets.
- Don't tag pro players.
- Don't claim "best coaching tool ever" — overpromise = backfire.
- Don't compare directly to specific competitors by name. "Most coaching tools are single-game" is fine; "Tool X sucks" is not.
