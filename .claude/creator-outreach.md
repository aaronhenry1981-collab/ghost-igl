hook — nothing ghostwritten, nothing disguised.

- Free Champion access for 6 months (their audience gets nothing gated away from what they saw)

**Rules:** send from aaron@ironfrontdigital.com, one at a time, personalize the first line
yourself if you've actually watched them. No follow-up before 7 days, one follow-up max.
Log every send below.

Aaron's machine (local server + his AWS keys + his capture setup). A creator cannot
install it today. Until a packaged creator build exists:
- **Pilot phase (now):** send to 1-2 small/patient creators only (Klaus64, BractionFPS).
